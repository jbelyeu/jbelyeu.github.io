I'm from outside of a small town named Kellyton in central Alabama, although I generally claim the slightly less small town of Alexander City (even though it's just as unknown). I moved to Provo, Utah when I was 18 to start school and haven't spent much time in 'Bama since.

Mission

I served as a volunteer missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from December of 2010 to December of 2012. I spent two years in the beautiful country of Colombia, learned Spanish, and still never got a tan.

Undergraduate Education

From late 2009 to early 2016 (with a break for the mission) I studied at Brigham Young University in the emerging field of Bioinformatics. I graduated with a BS in Bioinformatics (minor in Computer Science) in April 2016.

Graduate Education

I'm starting my graduate studies in Molecular Biology in August of 2016. I'm mostly interested in computational biology/bioinformatics and intend to funnel that into human genetics or possible oncological studies.
